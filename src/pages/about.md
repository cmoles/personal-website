### About Me, Personally

My name is Chris Moles. I'm a software engineer living in Dallas, Texas.
My main focus is in full stack development, with particular expertise in Python,
TypeScript, and C#/.NET. When I'm not coding, you might find me creating
[Pico-8 games](/games) or playing the guitar, ukulele, or synths.

### About Me, Professionally

I'm a product-minded full stack engineer with 17 years of experience shipping
software across startups and high-growth environments. I co-built an entire
fintech trading platform from the ground up as one of two engineers over five
years — owning frontend, backend, infrastructure, security, and payments
end-to-end. Most recently, I've been building AI-powered applications and
tooling, including an [MCP server for AI-assisted game development](/blog/pico-studio) and a
[job intelligence platform](/blog/job-search) with semantic search and LLM scoring.

My technical expertise includes:

- 💻 **Languages:** [Python](/tags/python), [TypeScript](/tags/typescript), C#/.NET, SQL
- 🌐 **Web & APIs:** [React](/tags/react), Angular, Flask, [FastAPI](/tags/fastapi), ASP.NET, REST APIs
- ☁️ **Cloud & Infra:** AWS, Azure, [Docker](/tags/docker), Linux
- 🤖 **AI & LLMs:** [Claude API](/tags/claude), [MCP servers](/tags/mcp), prompt engineering, vector search
- 📊 **Data & DevOps:** [SQLite](/tags/sqlite), SQLAlchemy, DataDog, CloudWatch, CI/CD, Git

### Technical Background

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of
Texas at Austin, with a focus on Image and Signal Processing, and Robotics and Controls.

My career spans multiple industries including fintech, political technology,
enterprise (Nvidia), satellite tracking, and electrical construction. I've
consistently led project delivery end-to-end, mentored developers, and driven
technical strategy alongside hands-on engineering.
