## Objective

Your book has so many pages for you to cast spells. They refill every time you enter a room. 


- Collect books to gain more pages
- More pages mean you can cast more spells.
- Collect spells to overcome obstacles.


You finish the game when you can transport away to continue to your next adventure.

## Items

- Gold key unlocks Locket.
- Silver key unlocks Door.
- Placed emerald unlocks Gate.
- Hammer breaks cracked Stone.

This game was made for Toy Box Jam 2025. I went for "hardcore" mode: making a game without changing any of the cart's assets, except for tile flags and extending the map.
