### Overview

Diving Deeper is a game about diving and exploring the ocean. You play as a diver who is exploring the ocean and trying to find treasure. You can explore the ocean floor, catch fish, and maybe save the day.

The game is currently in development.

### Objective

Collect as many points while you explore the ocean.

Points are currently under development.

### Controls

- Arrow keys to move
