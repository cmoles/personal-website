### Overview

Diving Deeper is a game about diving and exploring the ocean. You play as a diver who is exploring the ocean and trying to find treasure. You can explore the ocean floor, swim through caves, and even dive into the depths of the ocean. 

The game is currently in development.

### Objective

Collect as many points while you explore the ocean.

Points are currently under development.

### Controls

- Arrow keys to move
