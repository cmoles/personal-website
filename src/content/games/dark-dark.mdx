_You got a flashlight. Save your family._

### Controls:

- Left / Right to move;
- `X` to Swing Flashlight;
- Hold `X` to hold your direction;
- `Enter` / `P` to pause;

### Pause Menu Settings:

- Toggle Music;
- Blood Mode (green, red, purple, none);
