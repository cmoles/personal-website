_Command your own robot!_

### Controls:

- Arrow keys to move;
- `X` to toggle terminal;
- `O` / `Z` / `C` for actions;

### Terminal:

- `cls` to clear the screen;
- `debug` to get status of robot;
- `quit` to end game;
- `restart` to restart level;
- `wave` to send a wave of bugs;

### Game Modes:

- `story` to play the story;
- `free` to play your own way;
